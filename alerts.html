Owner's Audit Guide

The system automatically runs checks on draft and pending bills before approval. Alerts flag potential mistakes or fraud:

1Identify Anomalies

Look for Critical (price jumps, duplicate bills, total mismatches) or Warning flags.

2Audit and Rectify

Use "Review Bill" to inspect bill items. Resolve alerts by correcting data or rejecting incorrect bills.

3Approve Confidently

Clearing alerts ensures your inventory calculations, vendor ledger, and stock valuations remain accurate.
